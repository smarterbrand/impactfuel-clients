MOVING PEOPLE TO ACTION

Get it.

Believe it.

Take action.

if.

The first step to getting people to take action on your website is that they “get it”. They understand what you do and why you do it. This should happen within 10 seconds. If they don’t “get it”, they’re unlikely to stick around.

Once they “get it”, they’ll stay to learn more. They want to prove to themselves that you understand their problem and have the right people, process and experience to solve it. They want to “believe” you can actually make a difference for them.

And once they “believe it”, they’re ready to take the required steps to “get behind it”. They’re ready to make the call, complete the form, download the report, make the appointment and take action.

Our Web Solution

Impactfuel is recommending WordPress, an open source CMS.

wordpress.org

Why Wordpress?

  • Simple, easy-to-use CMS
  • Hundreds of thousands of users
  • Thousands of developers
  • Thousands of plugins
  • Built on PHP/MySQL
  • Can be hosted almost anywhere
  • Easy to upgrade
  • Well documented

Features and Functionality

  • Search engine friendly
  • Design flexibility
  • Full W3C standards compliance
  • Full-featured CMS (add, edit, remove pages)
  • Full-featured Blog
  • Password protected posts
  • Multiple users with 10 levels of permissions
  • Auto-generated sitemap
  • Auto-generated breadcrumb navigation

Why open source over a proprietary CMS.


Free: Open source software is free and carries no licensing fee.


Community & Flexibility: A large developer community means more plugins and support and you won’t be tied to the same developer for the life of your website.


Source Code/Customization: Complete access to the source code allows software modifications to fit the requirements of any project.


Stability & Performance: A broad installation base, tested extensively, and optimized through experience.


Security: In recent years WordPress has added the functionality to auto-update, which means you never need to worry.

CONTENT MARKETING PLAN

Podcasting.

Video.

Blogging.

Social.

Email.

Why podcasting? With today’s information overload, it’s one of the only platforms where you can get your audience’s attention for 10-40 minutes. It doesn’t require 100% focus like video or writing. You can multitask while listening.

Why video? Video is the most engaging form of media to get people’s attention quick. It’s also the best way to drive them to more in-depth content like your blog or podcast.

Why blogging? The best way to get found is still Google and Google still loves written content. Writing is hard, but transcribing an interview and posting it on your website is easy.

Why social media? Where people stay connected to their friends, their news, and their world. That said, it’s not enough to simply participate anymore. You need to actively solve their problems.

Why email? Email is still the most direct and measurable way to reach your audience. You can track what individual people open, what they click on, what they see on your website, and what they spend time with.
