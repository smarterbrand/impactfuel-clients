Section B · The Opportunity

Pittsburgh CRE is a
relationship market.
The internet got there first.

By the time a tenant calls about a 12,000-square-foot flex space in Lawrenceville, they've already read three things. Maybe one of them was yours. Probably none of them were.

The agents winning the second half of this decade aren't the loudest networkers. They're the ones whose name shows up when somebody Googles "absorption rate strip district" at 9pm on a Tuesday — and whose LinkedIn already answered the question before the meeting.

You already have the expertise. You don't have the time to type it.
That's a writing problem. And writing, done weekly, is what compounds.

Section G · The AI Part

About the AI part —
said plainly.

Half of agencies are quietly using AI and not telling you. The other half are loudly avoiding it and charging you for the hours. We're doing neither.

What AI Does

  • Transcribes your weekly interview.
  • Generates a structured first draft from the transcript.
  • Suggests three LinkedIn angles per post.
  • Catches things a tired human editor misses.

What AI Doesn't

  • Decide what we write about. You do.
  • Publish anything without your approval.
  • Replace the editing pass. John reads every word.
  • Make your voice sound like you. That's John's job.
